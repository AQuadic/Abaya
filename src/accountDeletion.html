Account Deletion Policy

Dear User,

Please make sure to read the account deletion policies carefully. You may delete your Ounass account at any time, but all the information linked to this account will be deleted permanently and are not recoverable later.

Active/Open Order: If you have an order which has yet to be delivered, or the order has been delivered, and its return period is still valid, it will still be considered an active order.Once a user deletes his account with an active order, they will only be able to track the order as a guest user on the Ounass platform and will need to contact the Customer Service team to initiate a return.

Order History: All the orders associated with your Ounass account will be treated as guest orders after successful account deletion. These orders can still be tracked as a guest user on the Ounass platform.

Wishlist:All the products will be removed from your Wishlist once your Ounass account has been deleted and are not recoverable.

Shopping bag/Cart:All products will be removed from your shopping bag/cart once your Ounass account has been deleted and are not recoverable.

Favourite designersAll Favourited Designers will be removed from the Favourite Designers list once the account is deleted.

Newsletter subscription:If you subscribe to our newsletter, you will be automatically unsubscribed once your account is deleted.
